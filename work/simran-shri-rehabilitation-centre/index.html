Simran Shri Rehabilitation Centre

Read more
Client:  Simran Shri Rehabilitation Centre
Year:  2025
Category:  Brand System · Local SEO · Social Media

Challenge

Simran Shri was operating in a high-intent, high-sensitivity category — rehabilitation and de-addiction — with almost no digital presence working in their favour. Call volume was low. Enquiry quality was poor. The brand offered exceptional care but nothing in their visual or digital presence communicated it.

Solution

Visual Systems rebuilt Simran Shri's brand presence from the ground up — visual identity, Google Business Profile, social content system, WhatsApp Business API integration, and CTWA ad configuration. Every touchpoint was redesigned around trust, credibility, and local search visibility.

Results

Significant increase in inbound call volume within the first 60 days. Enquiry quality improved measurably. Google Business Profile moved into the local Map Pack for high-intent rehabilitation search terms in Noida.
